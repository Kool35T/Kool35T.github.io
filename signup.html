About us

PikiPoko is a study website that hopes that each and every student in Singapore can receive all the learning materials that they want. We hope that all of our learners are kind-hearted and will always be willing to share their materials. Learning things can be hard, and it would be harder if one is lost and does not have any good materials to learn from. This website does not is advertisement free in order to not promote young people to buy things from online often.
